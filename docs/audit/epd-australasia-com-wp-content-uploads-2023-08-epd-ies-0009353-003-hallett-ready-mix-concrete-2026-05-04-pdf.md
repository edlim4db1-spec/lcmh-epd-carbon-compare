## Comparability notes
- Cradle-to-gate (A1-A3) + end-of-life (C1-C4) + module D EPD. A4, A5 and all B (use-stage) modules are NOT declared.
- This is a multi-product EPD. Products are manufactured at five South Australian batching plants (Dry Creek, Elizabeth, Mile End, McLaren Vale, Osborne). A1-A3 GWP is declared separately per plant and differs slightly by plant (e.g. N3220P Ref A1-A3 GWPt = 342 McLaren Vale, 344 Dry Creek, 346 Osborne, 349 Elizabeth). The A1-A3 values in this extraction are taken from the DRY CREEK plant results (page 11).
- Modules C1-C4 and D core GWP are declared ONLY for one representative mix, 'N3220P S50', with density 2 332 kg/m3 (page 35, Table 43). For all other mixes the C/D results are not separately printed; the EPD states they can be scaled linearly by density (Annex, printed page 70). C/D modules are therefore recorded only on the N3220P S50 product; on all other products C/D are omitted (not declared as printed values).
- Core GWP-total (GWPt) uses EN 15804+A2 characterisation. Note: the candidate JSON's C-module GWP values (14.7/5.65/4.93/8.33/-11.4) come from the ADDITIONAL indicator GWP-GHG (IPCC AR5, Table 46), not the core GWPt (14.8/5.96/5.02/8.45/-11.6, Table 43). This extraction records core GWPt.
- Enviro Construct low-carbon variants: F30/S30/S50/T50 are reduced-carbon mix designs (levels per Table 1, page 5); Ref is the reference mix with no Enviro Construct designation.
- Only the primary N-series product family (N2020P, N2520P, N3220P, N4020P, N5020P, strengths 20/25/32/40/50 MPa) and their Dry Creek A1-A3 variants were extracted. Many additional S-series and special-class mixes (S1020, S1520, S2520, S3220, S4020, S5010, S5020, S6510, S6520, S8010, CLSM, NOFINES, N2010P, etc.) are declared in the EPD but were NOT extracted (see needs_review).
